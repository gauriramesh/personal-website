Piano

Me and 88 Keys

I've been playing the piano since I was six years old and have loved it ever since. In my free time, I like to make piano covers of popular songs. I started doing this during my freshman year of high school. I started out with a 2012 classic, Payphone by Maroon 5. Another cover I made was of Paradise by Coldplay. My most recent work has been on the piece Unravel by Animenz. This one has been one of the hardest pieces I've prepared yet, and I'm excited to make a video and share it with you all.
