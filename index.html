Uh, so who am I?

Hi everyone! My name is Gauri Ramesh and I'm a student at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln majoring in Computer Science, with minors in Math and Business. I decided to make this website as a personal project over the summer of 2017 so I could learn more about web development. I'm trying to get my feet wet in everything so I can figure out what direction I want to take my computer science career in.

In my free time, I like to play the piano, write, read, and spend time with my loved ones. I also enjoy coaching high school speech and reading the news because I believe everyone's voice deserves to be heard.

People oftentimes find me outside on long walks or at coffee shops studying, coding, and listening to open mic nights.

I'm really passionate about people. As part of my long term career plans, I want to be a driving force in closing the gender gap in technology. Along with being a developer, I also would love to teach and work with kids to get them excited about technology and what they can do with it in the future.

Thanks for stopping by. Navigate the icons to learn more about what I do while the Earth spins.
