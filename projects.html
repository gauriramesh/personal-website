#HackUNL 2017 - Don't Stand By

The theme of HackUNL was Hack Harrassment, so my team and I built a Google Chrome extension and a website that allows users to report cyberbullying from any website. I will be working on developing a back end that processes the form information into a database and runs descriptive stats on it or refers the reporter to a place where they can get help for the cyberbullying victim.
